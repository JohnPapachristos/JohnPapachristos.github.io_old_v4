Workout program

  • Exercise 1:
    • Push ups(classic)
    • One of the greatest exercise that workouts chest, shoulders and core. This exercise can help you grow your arms because triceps are the biggest part of the hand.
    • 2-4 sets
    • 10-15 reps
  • Exercise 2:
    • Squats(classic)
    • The king of all exercises that works all the lower body but also the core.
    • 4-6 sets
    • 20-25 reps
  • Exercise 3:
    • Pull ups or australian pull ups
    • Both of the exercises are great to work the back and your biceps.
    • 2-4 sets
    • 5-15 reps
  • Exercise 4:
    • Push ups(diamond)
    • Great to improve the middle of the chest and also to build even bigger triceps.
    • 2-4 sets
    • 5-15 reps
  • Exercise 5:
    • Squats(Wide)
    • 4-6 sets
    • 20-25 reps
  • Exercise 6:
    • Pull ups or australian pull ups(Close hands to chest)
    • Great exercise to build your biceps.
    • 2-4 sets
    • 5-10 reps

    The certain program is for all levels, and can help you begin your fitness journey. My recommendation is to do it 3-4 times per week.
